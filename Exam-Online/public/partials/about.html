Tech-Arts eTest

We build the Tech-Arts eTest to facilitate candidates who don't have enough time go to our company and do a paper test.
A few tips and tricks before passing the test:

  • - the candidate must register basic information and an account
  • - the candidate login and choose the test corresponding the position you want to apply.
  • - the test has a time limit
  • - the test can be taken only once. So, you shouldn't leave test page until you're sure that your test was submitted.
  • - your score will be sent to you by e-mail after it is marked by admin
